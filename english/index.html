Our History

2016

Founded during the Rio 2016 Olympics, when we received the Meghalaya soccer delegation from India. Conducted training for the reception team at the Olympic Boulevard.

2020

Expanded tourism services to Doha, Qatar, serving Brazilians in transit between Brazil and Australia.

2022

Intensified reception activities, culminating with the Qatar World Cup, acting as a reference for reception in Doha.

2024

Added Dubai and Abu Dhabi to our service portfolio, winning over Brazilians and becoming a reference in airline ticket sales through points and city tour experiences.

2025

Formatted Doha service delivery focused on exchange students in Australia and intensified service to parents visiting their children in Oceania.

Tours

In our destinations, we work with 6, 8, and 10-hour tours. During this period, you choose what you want to visit, and we indicate the best way, either in the walking tour option or in cars with drivers and executive vans.

It all starts with an online chat, when we'll get to know more about you and develop a customized program that meets your preferences, your budget, and your expectations.

We optimize your time, with the best cost-benefit logistics, for you to get to know these destinations exactly as you wish. Whether it's a luxury experience or an economical experience.

Our tours are offered in:

  • Rio de Janeiro: Christ the Redeemer, Sugar Loaf, beaches, Tijuca Forest, and much more
  • Doha: Souq Waqif, Museum of Islamic Art, The Pearl, Katara Cultural Village
  • Dubai: Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, Palm Jumeirah, Dubai Marina, desert safari
  • Abu Dhabi: Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, Louvre Abu Dhabi, Ferrari World, Emirates Palace

Expatriates

Moving to a new country with family is a challenge that requires planning, support, and a reliable service network. Up Ex Patriates specializes in providing a smooth and personalized transition for expatriates in Rio de Janeiro, offering complete solutions that go beyond accommodation.

With a portfolio that includes rental of houses and apartments for temporary stays, assistance in choosing schools, selection and training of domestic staff, as well as boat rentals for leisure, we ensure a safe and comfortable experience.

An agency focused on expatriates needs to understand the cultural and operational needs of each family, offer bilingual and agile service, have a network of qualified suppliers, and, above all, provide security and practicality at each stage of the move. At Up, we combine expertise and hospitality so that our clients feel at home from day one in Brazil.

Our services for expatriates include:

  • Search and rental of residential properties
  • Guidance on international schools
  • Recruitment of domestic staff
  • Assistance with legal documentation
  • Cultural adaptation services
  • Continuous support throughout the stay
